The Telecommunications and Networking Laboratory (TN-Lab) is a dedicated research team concentrating on _wireless communications_ and the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning (AI/ML) in telecommunications and networking. 

This research team is led by Prof. [Tri Nhu Do](https://do-tri-nhu.info/), an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Polytechnique Montreal, Canada.
