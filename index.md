The Telecommunications and Networking Laboratory (TN-Lab) is a dedicated research group concentrating on wireless communications and the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning in telecommunications and networking. This laboratory is led by Prof. Tri Nhu Do, who holds the position of Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Polytechnique Montreal, located in Canada. The TN-Lab's research endeavors primarily focus on the development and enhancement of wireless communication technologies and the innovative application of AI/ML in this field.
